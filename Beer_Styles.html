There are many different styles of beer from around the world. Each beer style has a story and a history behind it as well. The best way to start understanding them is to start reading about the different styles of beer, and start tasting them. There are many resources on these topics there are a few that I go to the most.

The two resources I go to the most are from two large and well trusted organizations. They are detailed organized guidelines for styles of beer.

  • The Brewers Association puts out its yearly style guidelines here
  • The Beer Judge Certification Program has its guidelines with come commercially available examples of each style here
